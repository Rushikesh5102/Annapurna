Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 23, 2026

At Annapurna, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us directly, such as when you place an order, create an account, register as a chef, or contact us. This information includes your name, email address, phone number, shipping address, and payment options.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to process and track your orders, verify chef registrations, improve our menu catalog, send customer support alerts, and personalize your experience with Indian culinary traditions.

3. Local Storage and Cookies

We use browser local storage to maintain your interactive shopping cart contents locally. We do not use tracking cookies for third-party advertising profile collection.

4. Security of Your Data

We implement standard security measures to safeguard your details online. However, please remember that no transmission method over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please reach out to us at Rushikesh.pattiwar.@adypu.edu.in.
