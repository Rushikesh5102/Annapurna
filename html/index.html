Aloo Paratha

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 3 boiled potatoes
  • 1 small onion
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, amchur)
  • Ghee for cooking

Instructions

Knead dough, prepare stuffing, roll, cook on a tawa with ghee, and serve hot.

Rajma Chawal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rajma
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • Onions, tomatoes, and spices
  • Fresh coriander

Instructions

Cook rajma, prepare masala, simmer, and serve with steamed rice.

Gajar Ka Halwa

Ingredients

  • 1 kg carrots
  • 1 liter milk
  • Sugar, ghee, cardamom
  • Chopped nuts

Instructions

Sauté carrots in ghee, add milk, cook, sweeten, and garnish with nuts.
