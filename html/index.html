Featured Recipes

Aloo Paratha

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 3 boiled potatoes
  • 1 small onion
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, amchur)
  • Ghee for cooking

Instructions

Knead dough, prepare stuffing, roll, cook on a tawa with ghee, and serve hot.

Rajma Chawal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rajma
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • Onions, tomatoes, and spices
  • Fresh coriander

Instructions

Cook rajma, prepare masala, simmer, and serve with steamed rice.

Gajar Ka Halwa

Ingredients

  • 1 kg carrots
  • 1 liter milk
  • Sugar, ghee, cardamom
  • Chopped nuts

Instructions

Sauté carrots in ghee, add milk, cook, sweeten, and garnish with nuts.

Chole Bature

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chickpeas
  • 2 cups maida (flour)
  • Ginger, garlic, green chilies
  • Chole Masala powder
  • Yogurt & oil

Instructions

Knead maida with yogurt. Boil chole, prepare onion-tomato gravy, cook together, and serve with deep-fried bhature.

Masala Dosa

Ingredients

  • 3 cups raw rice
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • 4 boiled potatoes
  • Curry leaves & mustard seeds

Instructions

Ferment rice batter. Prepare spiced potato filling. Spread batter thin on griddle, fold over potatoes, and serve hot.

Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer cubes
  • 2 onions & 3 tomatoes
  • Butter & heavy cream
  • Cashews & spices

Instructions

Puree onions, tomatoes, and cashews. Cook in butter with spices, add paneer, simmer, finish with cream.
