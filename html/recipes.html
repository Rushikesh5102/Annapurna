Butter Chicken Recipe:
Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices. Cook in butter, add tomato puree, cream, and spices. Serve hot with naan or rice.
Masala Dosa Recipe:
Ferment rice-lentil batter. Spread on a griddle, fill with spiced potato mixture. Serve with coconut chutney and sambar.
Rogan Josh Recipe:
Sauté lamb in oil with onions, garlic, and ginger. Add yogurt, Kashmiri chili, and spices. Simmer until tender. Enjoy with naan.
Biryani Recipe:
Layer spiced meat and parboiled rice in a pot. Seal and cook on low heat. Serve with raita or salad.
Rajma Chawal Recipe:
Boil kidney beans, then cook in a tomato-based gravy with spices. Serve over steamed rice.
Paneer Tikka Recipe:
Marinate paneer cubes in spiced yogurt. Grill or bake until golden. Serve with mint chutney.
Gulab Jamun Recipe:
Make dough from khoya, fry into golden balls. Soak in warm sugar syrup flavored with rose water.
Chole Bhature Recipe:
Cook chickpeas in spiced gravy. Make dough, roll, and deep-fry for fluffy bread. Serve together.
Puran Poli Recipe:
Prepare jaggery-lentil stuffing. Roll dough, fill, and flatten. Cook on a griddle with ghee.
Masala Chai Recipe:
Boil water with ginger, cardamom, and tea leaves. Add milk and sugar, simmer, strain, and serve hot.
